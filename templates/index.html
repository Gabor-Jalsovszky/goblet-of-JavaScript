Harry Potter and the Goblet of JavaScript
Instruction

You have to get the Goblet in order to win.

You are the deathly hallows symbol.

You can use the key symbol to open the door.

You can use the water symbol to get through the fire symbol.

You can use the bomb symbol to destroy 4 walls next to you.

You can destroy the dementor by pressing 'x' when it is under you.

If the dementor is next to you, and it reaches you, you die and you have to start over if you want.
